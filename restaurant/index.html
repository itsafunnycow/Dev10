What will you find at DR's?
DR's is a casual restuarant founded on the simple premise of providing patrons with quality meals. Founder, Derek Clement, always cherished interesting tastes and a full stomach. DR's goal is to bring unique and tasty flavors to your plate. The menu items offered have been collected naturally over years of enjoying the creations of other chefs and experimenting in the kitchen. Please come on in and experience the flavors that inspired these additions to our menu.
